The Difference Between Fans and Followers

John 12:12-19

   |  

Chad Hays

What Does God Want Me to Know?

A fan of Jesus has an earthly and selfish view of what Jesus can do for them.

A follower of Jesus has a selfless and eternal view of what Jesus can do for them and through them.

Fans are not willing to give up anything for Jesus.

Followers realize there is no price too high to pay for following Jesus.

Fans see a wide road to salvation.

Followers realize that the road to salvation is very narrow and extremely exclusive.

The Bottom Line

Jesus never said, “Be my fan.” He said, “Come follow me.”
